How It Works

Revolutionary AI technology for oral cancer detection

Our Technology

We use advanced deep learning algorithms trained on thousands of medical images to detect early signs of oral cancer with remarkable accuracy.

Our Detection Process

1

Image Capture

Take or upload a clear image of the oral cavity area using your smartphone or camera.

  • Ensure good lighting
  • Capture the entire area of concern
  • Avoid blur and shadows
2

AI Analysis

Our deep learning model processes the image using advanced computer vision techniques.

  • Image preprocessing and enhancement
  • Feature extraction using CNN
  • Pattern recognition and classification
3

Classification

The AI classifies the image into three categories with confidence scores.

  • Normal: No suspicious findings
  • Suspicious: Requires medical attention
  • Cancer: Immediate medical consultation needed
4

Instant Results

Receive comprehensive analysis with confidence scores and recommendations.

  • Primary classification result
  • Confidence percentage
  • Probability breakdown
  • Recommended next steps
